data TerminalType Source #

Constructors

Ansi16Colour

\033[Xm-style escape sequences (with X =~ [34][0-7])

XTerm256Compatible

Ansi16Colour, and also \033[Y8;5;Zm]-style escape sequences (with Y =~ [3,4] and Z an integer in [0,255] with the XTerm colour cube semantics).
